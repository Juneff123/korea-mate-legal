Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 23, 2026 · Korea Mate

Korea Mate (the "App") is operated by Yuwol Film (유월필름). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information — when you sign in with Apple, Google, or email, we collect your email address and name.
  • Booking requests — details you provide for a reservation (service category, preferred date/time, party size, special requests, and any photos you attach).
  • Messages — communications between you and our concierge team.
  • Device & usage data — basic technical data (device type, language, app version) to operate and improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and coordinate your reservation requests with businesses (restaurants, beauty & wellness providers).
  • To communicate with you about your bookings via in-app chat.
  • To provide customer support and improve the App.

3. Sharing

To arrange your booking, we share only the necessary details (such as your name, party size, and preferred time) with the business you ask us to book. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for advertising.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (data center in Seoul, South Korea) with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest, and strict access controls.

5. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your data at any time. The App includes an in-app "Delete Account" option that permanently removes your account and associated data. You can also email us at 106ljune@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

Korea Mate is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
