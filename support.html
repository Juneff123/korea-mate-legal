What is Korea Mate?

Korea Mate is a booking concierge for visitors to Korea. Tell us what you'd like to book — a restaurant, a salon, and more — and our team coordinates directly with the business and confirms everything for you through in-app chat.

FAQ

How do I make a booking request?

Open the app, choose a category, fill in your preferred date, time and party size, then submit. Our team takes it from there.

How much does it cost?

Korea Mate is currently free to use — there is no platform fee.

What languages are supported?

The app is available in English, Korean, Japanese, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Vietnamese, Thai, Indonesian, Spanish, and Russian. You can switch anytime in settings.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data.
